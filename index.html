HBG Supply LLC - Warehouse Management System (WMS) Project

Introduction

This project proposes a three-step plan to develop a comprehensive Warehouse Management System (WMS) for HBG Supply LLC, focused on optimizing operations and stock control of PVC tubes. Leveraging Python, Streamlit, Supabase, and PowerBI, the system will provide a robust, scalable, and user-friendly platform.

Key Technologies:

  • Python: Backend development, data processing, and system integration.
  • Streamlit: Interactive user interface for managing and visualizing stock data.
  • Supabase: Database management, real-time data synchronization, and authentication.
  • PowerBI: Advanced dashboards and reports for data analysis and decision-making.

Step 1: Prototype Implementation and Improvement

Objective: Validate the initial prototype and ensure smooth operation for stock tracking of PVC tubes stored in DGM-Florida.

  • Duration: 1 month
  • Key Activities:
    • Kick-off meeting to present the prototype and underlying technologies (Python, Streamlit, Supabase).
    • Deploy and test the module for tracking stocks at DGM-Florida.
    • Collect user feedback and implement improvements.
  • Deliverables:
    • Fully functional stock tracking module.
    • Comprehensive report on feedback and planned improvements.
    • Final meeting to discuss outcomes, review the prototype, and agree on next steps.

Step 2: Expanded Development for Full Stock Control

Objective: Develop a fully-featured WMS for tracking all HBG operations and stock.

  • Duration: 2 months
  • Key Activities:
    • Develop modules for managing stocks from hubs located in Nashville, Tampa, and Greensboro.
    • Implement modules for inbound and outbound stock management for all hubs.
    • Integrate advanced reporting and analytics tools, including dashboards in Streamlit and PowerBI.
    • Enhance user interface and security features.
    • Host mid-project meeting to evaluate progress and prioritize additional features.
  • Deliverables:
    • Comprehensive WMS covering all stock control needs.
    • Access to dashboards in Streamlit and PowerBI.
    • Training sessions for HBG staff.
    • Mid-project meeting to present a progress review and roadmap for finalization.

Step 3: System Optimization and Maintenance

Objective: Refine the WMS and ensure it meets long-term operational needs.

  • Duration: 1 month
  • Key Activities:
    • Optimize system performance and scalability.
    • Implement additional features based on user feedback.
    • Establish a maintenance plan and detailed documentation.
    • Host a final review meeting to summarize outcomes and present the enhanced system.
  • Deliverables:
    • Enhanced WMS with tailored features for HBG Supply LLC.
    • Detailed maintenance and user manuals.
    • Final review meeting to transfer ownership and align on ongoing support.

Conclusion

This phased approach ensures a robust and scalable WMS tailored to HBG Supply LLC's needs. By focusing on incremental improvements, we can achieve high user satisfaction and a sustainable solution.
